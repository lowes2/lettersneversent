LETTERS I NEVER SENT

                

                
                An album by Louis Wang, designed entirely within Musescore 4.7.

                I hope you all enjoy my music! 

                
                

                    
About the Album

                

                
                Letters I Never Sent is sort of based around this guy who,
                like in most songs, meets a girl, blah blah blah, and for whatever
                reason they have to seperate. From there, the album explores the 
                many feelings associated with loss. 

                Some parts of this album mean a lot to me, so I hope
                you enjoy listening as much as I've enjoyed writing it.
